In the United States, there are currently over 2 Million wounded veterans... men and women that have risked everything for our freedom.

From physical trauma to PTSD, For many readjustment back into the civilian life has proven difficult to not only themselves but also their families.

As a marine, I know the unique skills and training our military members receive. And as photographers, we've used this training to discover a new method of viewing and capturing the world around us.

At One Shot One Skill, we see a way to help our warriors by equipping them with a new weapon for life. We've developed a program and curriculum that helps our vets to take their military and combat training and apply it directly to photography and the Arts Empowering them with RENEWED purpose, vision and source of income.

We are seeking $100,000 from individuals to corporations to get through our first year of operations...

With those funds we will be equipping veterans with cameras, laptops, and training, the completion of an E-learning platform and establishing a sustainable social enterprise.

We are also looking for mentors... photographers who want to make a difference in our veterans lives.

And Most importantly, the veterans themselves. If interested in any of these areas, sign up below for more info.
