What will you learn?

1

No more Wantrepreneur

An entrepreneur is someone who makes things happen, a WANTREPRENEUR is someone who just TALKS about making things happen. You'll learn how to quickly get over this and take action like a BOSS.
2

Finding a hungry market

There are riches in niches. (cheesey but true). You'll learn why niche markets are so powerful and how to find them.
3

Testing the problem

So many new entrepreneurs spend months building things without talking to a SINGLE customer. You'll learn why getting out and talking to customers is crucial to early success.
4

Testing the solution

Does your product or idea solve a painful problem? If it does, will a customer pay for your solution? You'll learn how to validate an idea before spending any money.
5

Creating an MVP

The goal of an MVP (Minimum Viable Product) is learn as quickly as possible and will allow you gather a ton of information on what works and what doesn't. You'll learn how to create your own MVP.
6

Pivot or Persevere

The goal may never change, but the tactics to get there always change. You'll learn when to keep pushing forward and when it's time to move in another direction.
7

Getting it built

So once you've proved the concept using your MVP, it's now time to start building the real thing. You'll learn how to find developers/designers, or how to build it yourself.
8

Grow the heck out of it

Most new entrepreneurs will take a "spray and pray" approach to marketing. They'll spam their friends, and spends thousands on business cards and billboards. #fail. You'll learn how inbound marketing is MUCH more effective and valuable than outbound marketing (and what the heck those terms mean).

All you need is a burning desire to learn.

You don't need any business/tech background, fancy suits, high IQ, or connections.
